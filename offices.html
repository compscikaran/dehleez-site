"It's all about finding the calm in the chaos."
Donna Karan

The corporate world is generally perceived as a ruthless de-humanised zone, a place which one would love to hate. Architecture can become the knight in shining armour by abetting the humanising of spaces, within the framework of the rigour and discipline that is intrinsic to this part of the world. The challenge lies in achieving this within the constraints of space and without. Most of our corporate clients are those who have been with us since their inception and have grown, like we have. Thus, there is a tacit understanding of the work culture, the processes and the protocol. The approach has been to adhere to the degree of formality required in the specific activity areas, but to use interaction zones and circulation paths as accidental pause spaces in compliance with scale, light and nature where one can let one's hair down and introspect if need be.
